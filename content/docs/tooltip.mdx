### Variants

Tooltips support `default` and `destructive` variants. The default arrow is a small rotated square; the destructive arrow is a triangular caret (same surface fill as the bubble, no border on the tip) so it does not read as a heavy diamond.

The destructive bubble uses `border: 1px solid var(--Outline-danger_base_em_alpha, rgba(237, 64, 48, 0.20))` and `background: var(--Surface-danger_base_em_alpha, rgba(237, 64, 48, 0.12))`, with `text-destructive` for the label.

### Alignment

You can use the `align` property to align the tooltip (and its arrow) to the `start` (left), `center`, or `end` (right) relative to the trigger.
